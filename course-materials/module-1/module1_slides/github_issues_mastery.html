Mastering Issue Management

GitHub Issues Are the Command Center for Project Management

Issues are not just bug reports—they are tasks, features, discussions, and documentation all in one centralized location.
Professional issue management includes: descriptive titles, detailed descriptions, appropriate labels, clear acceptance criteria, and linked pull requests.
Issue templates standardize information collection and reduce back-and-forth communication, ensuring all necessary details are captured upfront.
Labels enable filtering and prioritization: bug, enhancement, documentation, high-priority, good-first-issue—creating an organized taxonomy for your work.
Milestones group related issues for sprint or release planning, providing clear progress visibility and helping teams stay aligned on goals.
